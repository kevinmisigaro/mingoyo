About Us

Mingoyo Sawmill Company Limited was established in 1975 owned by the Government of Tanzania through its parastatal vehicle of investment Tanzania Wood Industry Corporation (TWICO). Later in 1996 it was privatized as a going concern manufacturing wood products and acquired by a private entrepreneur Mr. Issa M. Rwabusimbi. Under the private investor it was incorporated, renovated and installed with machinery and equipment. Currently the company is manufacturing semi-finished component for music instruments mainly clarinets and bag pipes. In 2007 Maridadi Timberworks Limited was established as a subsidiary company of Mingoyo Sawmill Company Limited mainly dealing with manufacturing of all types of hardwood home and office furniture.

In 2014 Mingoyo Sawmill Co. Ltd. further expanded and established another sawmill in Morogoro Region. In the same year 2014, Maridadi’s Showroom Ltd. was established. This is also a subsidiary company of Mingoyo Sawmill Co. Ltd. and it is specifically dealing with all types of furniture and distribution and sales all types of interior Products. As well it represents German companies in Tanzania e. g. Bosch (Home Appliances), Grohe (sanitary fittings), Taps&More (sanitary fittings), Dorma (door fittings).

In 2013 - 2014 Mingoyo Sawmill Company Limited experienced a significant growth of its operations with ultimate results of incorporating and thus changing its name to MINGOYO GROUP OF COMPANIES controlling the following companies:

  1. Mingoyo Sawmill Company Limited
  2. Maridadi Timber Works Limited
  3. Maridadi’s Showroom Limited
